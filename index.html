This is an interactive data visualisation of the number π. It's a derivative work based on the 2013 π-day poster by Martin Krzywinski, http://mkweb.bcgsc.ca/pi/piday.

Tap or click on the number symbols in the top to choose a subject. Drag the sliders to change the density of the circles or the number of columns. Tap or click the image to toggle connecting lines.

Source code available on GitHub.
